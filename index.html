Berkling 18mm Utility Knife with 4x SK2H Blades and Heavy-Duty Aluminum Frame

This 18mm retractable utility knife is the perfect tool for DIYers, hobbyists, and professionals alike. The sturdy yet lightweight aluminum body ensures both durability and ease of use. The SK2H black blade is 4x tougher than standard SK5 blades and offers superior anti-corrosion properties. It can also be sharpened multiple times, making it a long-lasting tool.

This utility knife is ideal for a variety of professionals, including plumbers, electricians, mechanics, and carpenters. The comfortable anti-slip handle allows for easy one-handed control, and the three extra heavy-duty, razor-sharp blades safely store inside the knife. To replace the used blade, just simply push out the dull blade and the tool will replace it with a fresh one stored inside the handle.

The retractable design is ideal for heavy-duty projects and offers both safety and portability without compromising performance. This 18mm retractable utility knife is perfect for use as a pocket knife, box cutter, carpet cutter, razor knife, or even a razor blade utility knife. With 4x SK2H blackened and hardened blades, this utility knife allows you to complete tasks quickly and efficiently.

  • RETRACTABLE - The retractable design of this 18mm utility knife ensures safer use and prevents accidental cuts when not in use
  • SK2H BLACKENED BLADE - Our 18mm retractable utility knife features an SK2H blackened and hardened carbon steel blade that's sharper and more durable than other knives with SK5 blades. Plus, it offers superior corrosion resistance
  • SNAPP-OFF BLADE - The 18mm snap-off blade has 7 sections that can be easily snapped off to reveal a fresh, sharp blade section for use when one gets dull
  • SELF-LOADING BLADE - The auto-change mechanism inside the handle can hold up to 3 spare blades, allowing for safe, quick and easy blade changes without touching the blade.
  • DURABLE & SKID-PROOF - Constructed with a durable heavy-duty aluminum alloy body and skid-proof handle, this utility knife is rust-proof and corrosion-resistant, making it ultra-durable for any cutting job
  • SAFETY LOCKING MECHANISM - This 18mm retractable knife keeps you safe while using it by automatically locking the blade in place. It also prevents the blade from extending when not in use.
  • MULTI-PURPOSE - This multi-purpose utility knife is perfect for cutting cardboard, linoleum, carpet, plastics, and other materials. Suitable for both home and industrial applications, it's ideal for cutting, trimming, and shaping a wide variety of materials.
